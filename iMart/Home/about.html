About Our Mart

What began little, with a solitary idea of a store that provides a rebate and the basic thought of moving more for less, has become in the course of the last 10 years into the biggest retailer in this whole country.
Every week, about 100 Thousand clients and individuals visit our in excess of 600 stores under 55 standards in 6 nations and eCommerce sites in 8 nations.
With the financial year 2018 income of $500.3 million i-Mart utilizes over 1.5 million partners around the world.
i-Mart keeps on being an innovator in supportability, corporate magnanimity, and work opportunity.
It's everything part of our relentless promise to make openings and convey an incentive to clients and networks the world over.
